Our Founding

Delish by Arlene Hernandez Balba is a Davao-based dessert shop specializing in exquisite pastries and baked goods. Established in 2020, Delish by Arlene Hernandez Balba is the product of passion and dedication in crafting confections. Each piece is hand-baked and crafted with personal touches to suit each customer's taste. Every piece is assured high-quality with its ingredients. At Delish by Arlene Hernandez Balba, customer satisfaction is the ultimate goal. We strive to satisfy sweet cravings and incite happiness with each bite. Limited-time goods and special requests are entertained and delivered with the highest guarantee.

Our Future

At Delish, we envision a future filled with delectable moments and sweet celebrations. Our goal is to continue crafting irresistible desserts that not only satisfy your taste buds but also create lasting memories. With a commitment to quality ingredients, artisanal craftsmanship, and a passion for perfection, we aim to be a beloved destination for those seeking a symphony of flavors in every bite. As we look ahead, we see a community where our desserts become synonymous with joy, bringing people together to savor life's sweetest moments. Our vision is to be more than just a dessert shop; we strive to be a cherished part of your celebrations, elevating the experience with our divine treats.
